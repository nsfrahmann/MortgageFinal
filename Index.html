Here's the magic behind the scenes:

Loan = Home Price - Down Payment

Current Balance = Loan - Principal Payment

Principal Payment = Monthly Payment - Interest Payment

Interest Payment = Balance x (Interest Rate / 1200)

Your Monthly Payment = (Loan x Interest Rate / 1200) / {1 - [1 + (Interest Rate / 1200)^(-Term in Years x 12) ] }

Home Price (with 20% Down Payment) you can get based on what you can afford monthly:

Target Home Price = 1.25 x ( {Monthly Payment x {1 - [1 + (Interest Rate / 1200)^(-Term in Years x 12) ] } ) / (Interest Rate / 1200)
